MuseScore PDF Exporter

v1.0.0

How to use:

  1. Open a sheet music page on musescore.com.
  2. Look for the floating "Clean PDF Export" button in the bottom right corner of the page.
  3. Click it and wait for all the preview pages to load.
  4. In the system print popup, set destination to "Save as PDF".
  5. Important: Uncheck "Headers and footers" for a perfectly clean PDF!
